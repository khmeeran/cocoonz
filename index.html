Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents ask us before joining Cocoonz

Preschool (Pre-KG to UKG): 9:00 AM – 12:30 PM (Extended hours until 5:00 PM available).

NextGen Primary (Grades 1-5): 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Cocoonz NextGen is our Primary Section for Grades 1-5. It features smart classrooms, dedicated STEM and Math labs, spoken French, chess, and a balanced curriculum focused on both academic excellence and holistic development. Timings are 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM. Learn more →

We accept children from Pre-KG to Grade 1. Our programs include Pre-KG, LKG, UKG, and Grade 1 — each with a dedicated, age-appropriate curriculum that builds strong foundations for lifelong learning.

Enrollment is simple! Visit your nearest branch for a campus tour, fill out the registration form, and submit your child's birth certificate and a few passport photos. Admissions are open year-round, subject to seat availability.

Safety is our top priority. All campuses are CCTV monitored with live feeds. We have child-friendly furniture with rounded edges, secured gates, fire extinguishers, and trained staff in first aid. Only authorized parents/guardians can pick up children.

Yes! We serve nutritious, freshly prepared meals including morning snacks, lunch, and evening snacks. Our menu is planned by nutritionists to ensure balanced, healthy meals. We also accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies — just let us know.

Absolutely! We welcome parents to visit during school hours. Just inform the front desk and we'll arrange a guided tour. We also hold regular parent-teacher meetings, open house days, and special events where you can see your child in action.

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio of approximately 1:10 to ensure every child gets individual attention. Our experienced educators are trained in early childhood development and are passionate about nurturing young minds.

We follow an Emergent, Theme-Based Curriculum that blends play-based learning with early academics. Our approach includes phonics, activity-based math, creative arts, music, yoga, and life skills — all designed to make learning joyful and meaningful.

Our staff is trained in first aid and every campus has a first aid kit. In case of illness or injury, we immediately contact the parent/guardian. If needed, we arrange transport to the nearest medical facility. We also have tie-ups with nearby clinics for quick response.

Yes, we offer safe, GPS-tracked transportation in select areas. Our buses and vans are equipped with seat belts, trained attendants, and CCTV. Routes and availability vary by branch — please contact your nearest branch for route details.

Yes, we follow a structured academic calendar with holidays for national festivals, summer break, and winter break. Extended hours remain available during most school holidays to support working parents. We share the yearly calendar at the start of each academic year.

We believe in positive reinforcement and gentle guidance, never punishment. Our teachers use redirection, empathy-building, and age-appropriate conversations to help children understand boundaries. We work closely with parents to support each child's emotional growth.
